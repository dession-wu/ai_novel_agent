## Color Palette Suggestions

### Primary Colors
- **Blue**: #1E40AF (deep blue)
- **Purple**: #7C3AED (vibrant purple)
- **Gradient**: Blue to Purple

### Secondary Colors
- **Cyan**: #06B6D4 (for accents)
- **Pink**: #EC4899 (for creative elements)

## Design Considerations

### Style Guidelines
- **Flat design** with subtle gradients
- **Clean, minimalist approach**
- **No text** (logo only)
- **Vector format** for scalability
- **Professional appearance** (unicorn startup vibe)

### Symbolism
- **Book**: Represents writing and literature
- **Brain/Neural Network**: Represents AI intelligence
- **Glow effect**: Represents innovation and cutting-edge technology
- **Gradient**: Represents creativity and transformation

### Usage Recommendations
- **File formats**: SVG (primary), PNG (transparent), JPG
- **Sizes**: 512x512, 1024x1024, 2048x2048
- **Applications**: Website, GitHub, social media, marketing materials

### Inspiration References
- **Notion**: Clean, minimalist design
- **Slack**: Simple, recognizable symbol
- **OpenAI**: Modern tech aesthetic
- **Figma**: Creative, approachable design

## Final Output Requirements
- **High resolution** (minimum 1024x1024)
- **Transparent background** (for versatility)
- **Vector format** (for scalability)
- **Professional appearance** (suitable for investment pitches)
- **Unique design** (not derivative of existing logos)
