## Getting Started

1. Open the application in your browser (default: `http://localhost:5173`).
2. You will see the **Dashboard** which lists your current novels.

## Creating a Novel

1. Click the **"Create New Novel"** (创建新小说) button on the dashboard.
2. Fill in the basic information:
   - **Title**: The name of your book.
   - **Genre**: E.g., Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Mystery.
   - **Style**: E.g., Cyberpunk, Dark, Humorous.
   - **Synopsis**: A brief description of what the story is about.
3. Click **Submit**. Your novel will appear in the list with a status of "Planning".

## Generating an Outline

1. Click on your newly created novel card to enter the **Novel Detail** view.
2. Click the **"Generate Outline"** button.
3. The AI will analyze your synopsis and generate a structured outline, including character profiles and a chapter-by-chapter plot summary.
4. You can review and edit the outline before proceeding.

## Writing Chapters

1. In the Novel Detail view, scroll to the **Chapter List**.
2. Click **"Add New Chapter"** (添加新章节).
3. Enter the chapter title and a brief plot point (or use the one from the outline).
4. Click **"Generate Content"**.
   - The AI will write the chapter content (~2000 words), considering the context of previous chapters.
5. Click **"Edit"** to open the Editor. You can refine the text using the Markdown editor.

## Proofreading

1. Inside the Editor, click the **"Proofread"** (校稿) button in the toolbar.
2. The system will scan your text for:
   - Grammar errors
   - Sensitive words
   - Logic inconsistencies
3. Review the suggestions and apply changes as needed.
4. Once satisfied, you can change the chapter status to **"Finalized"**.

## Tips for Best Results

- **Be Specific**: The more detailed your synopsis and style description, the better the AI can match your vision.
- **Iterate**: Don't be afraid to regenerate a chapter if the first draft isn't perfect. You can also edit the prompt for specific scenes.
